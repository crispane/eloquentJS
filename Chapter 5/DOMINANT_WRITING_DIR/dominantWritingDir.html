Write a function that computes the dominant writing direction in a string of text. Remember that each script object has a direction prop- erty that can be "ltr" (left to right), "rtl" (right to left), or "ttb" (top to bottom). The dominant direction is the direction of a majority of the char- acters that have a script associated with them. The characterScript and countBy functions defined earlier in the chapter are probably useful here.
